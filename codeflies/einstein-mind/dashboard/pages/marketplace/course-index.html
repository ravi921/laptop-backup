How to buy TalentLibrary
TalentLibrary is subscription-based. To access it, upgrade your TalentLMS plan to Include TalentLibrary and you'll have unlimited access to all courses. Once upgraded, add courses to your Course Catalog to use.
How to buy courses by other providers
Course providers use a licensing model. You can license individual courses a la carte, or buy a bundle license that gives you access to select subsets of a provider's library. As soon as you buy licenses for paid courses, they are automatically added to your Course Catalog.
